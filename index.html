01 — About

What is a Sherpa?

In Escape From Tarkov, a Sherpa is an experienced player who guides newer or struggling players through the brutal learn-by-dying loop that defines the game.

🗺️

Map Knowledge

Extract locations, loot routes, high-traffic hotspots, chokepoints — we know every map like the back of our rig glove. Stop spawning blind.

🎯

Combat Coaching

Audio cues, peeker's advantage, when to push, when to hold. Learn positioning and crosshair placement mid-raid instead of on YouTube.

📋

Quest Guidance

Stuck on a quest chain for 3 raids? We know every spawn, item location, and trick to knock out quest objectives fast and painlessly.

📦

Loot & Economy

What's worth grabbing, what to skip, hideout priorities, flea market flipping. Build your stash faster with smarter decisions.
